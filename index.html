Calling all Burning Globes: now is the time to travel the globe! CJU currently offers study abroad experiences in Italy, France, England, and Australia. Explore your options today!

Take up to 12 credits during the offered 6-8 week courses. Plan your curriculum to learn a new language, try tasty cooking and cuisine, and study the country's vibrant culture.

For additional information on offered courses and credit hours, contact a study abroad advisor.

NOTICE: All study abroad programs adhere to COVID-19 travel restrictions. Learn more here.
