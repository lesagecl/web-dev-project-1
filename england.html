Visit London

Live in and explore London, Britain's most cosmopolitan city! Walk amongst the picturesque streets while marveling at the modern architecture. Visit popular attractions such as Buckingham Palace, Westminster Abbey, and St. Paul's Cathedral. Learn about British government while visiting the Houses of Parliament. We can guarantee your schedule will be booked! Most classes have weekend outings students may attend even if they are not enrolled in that class.

Curriculum

ARTH314: Navigate through historic interpretations of European art. Develop a lense for extrapolating meaning from art through cultural and historcal context. Gain skills in critical analysis. 3 credits.

POSC220: The history of modern politics in the United Kingdom. Understand the framework of a parliamentary democracy beneath a constitutional monarchy. Distinguish between the responsibilities of the head of state versus the prime minister. 3 credits.

HIST137: England in the Middle Ages. Explore topics of the medieval period up until the Early Modern period. Research how Germanic migration shifted the cultural identity of England. Includes a trip to the Museum of London. 3 credits.

HUM216: Build an appreciation for the folk music of the United Kingdom. Study the creation of folk music and how it flourished up until the industrial revolution. Compare the sounds of England, Northern Ireland, Scotland, and Wales. Understand how music spreads and creates fusions of subgenres over time. 3 credits.
